# 🪂 Airdrop Hunter Tips

## Maximizing Airdrop Returns

1. **Start early** — The earlier you use a protocol, the more likely you are to qualify for retroactive airdrops.

2. **Volume matters** — Don't just make one $1 swap. Make meaningful transactions over time.

3. **Bridge across chains** — Many L2 airdrops reward bridging activity. Use the official bridge.

4. **Governance participation** — Vote on proposals. Protocols love engaged users.

5. **Provide liquidity** — Even small LP positions show protocol loyalty.

## Red Flags (Scam Airdrops)

- Asks for your private key or seed phrase
- Requires you to "approve" a suspicious contract
- Sends unsolicited tokens to your wallet (dusting attack)
- Website URL is slightly misspelled
- No official announcement from the protocol

## Wallet Hygiene

- Use a separate wallet for airdrop farming
- Never connect your main wallet to unknown dApps
- Revoke unused approvals regularly (revoke.cash)
- Use a hardware wallet for high-value holdings
