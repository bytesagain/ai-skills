# FitLog

Track every workout. See your progress. Build streaks. FitLog is a simple fitness journal for your terminal.

## Why FitLog?

- **Quick logging**: Record any workout in one command
- **Multiple types**: Run, swim, bike, gym, yoga, HIIT, and more
- **Streak tracking**: Build and maintain exercise streaks
- **Weekly overview**: See active days and total training time
- **Statistics**: Track your favorite activities and total volume

## Commands

- `log <type> <minutes> [notes]` — Log a workout session
- `today` — View today's workouts
- `week` — Weekly fitness summary with breakdown by type
- `stats` — Overall fitness statistics
- `streak` — Current exercise streak
- `info` — Version info
- `help` — Show commands

## Workout Types

Run, walk, swim, bike, gym, yoga, hiit, other — or any custom type you want.

## Usage Examples

```bash
fitlog log run 30 morning jog in the park
fitlog log gym 45 upper body day
fitlog log yoga 20
fitlog week
fitlog streak
```
