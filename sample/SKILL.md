# Sample — Statistical Sampling Reference

Quick-reference skill for sampling methods, sample size calculations, and experimental design.

## Commands

### `intro`
Overview of statistical sampling — why sample, population vs sample.
### `methods`
Sampling methods — simple random, stratified, cluster, systematic.
### `size`
Sample size calculations — confidence level, margin of error, effect size.
### `bias`
Sampling bias types — selection, survivorship, response, nonresponse.
### `ab_test`
A/B test design — hypothesis, power analysis, duration, significance.
### `reservoir`
Reservoir sampling — sampling from streams of unknown size.
### `bootstrap`
Bootstrap and resampling methods — confidence intervals without assumptions.
### `quality`
Sample quality — representativeness, weighting, and post-stratification.
