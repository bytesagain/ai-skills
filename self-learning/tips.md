# Self Learning — Tips

1. **Reflect after every major task** — Don't wait for end-of-day. Quick reflections while context is fresh are more valuable.

2. **Score honestly** — Self-evaluation only works if you're objective. Rate your actual output, not your intent.

3. **Set 2-3 goals max** — Too many goals dilute focus. Pick the highest-impact improvements.

4. **Journal consistently** — Even a one-line entry beats skipping a day. Consistency builds the data set.

5. **Extract insights weekly** — Let patterns emerge over 5-7 days of journal entries before analyzing.

6. **Track habits with streaks** — Visible streaks create positive reinforcement loops.

7. **Benchmark quarterly** — Compare 3-month windows for meaningful performance trends.
