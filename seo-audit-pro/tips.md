## Quick Tips

1. **Start with `score`** — Get a quick SEO score before diving into detailed analysis
2. **Full audit for reports** — Use `audit` command to generate comprehensive HTML reports for clients
3. **Check headings first** — Heading hierarchy issues are the most common and easiest to fix
4. **Alt text matters** — Missing image alt text hurts both SEO and accessibility
5. **Mobile audit** — Use `--mobile` flag to audit from a mobile user-agent perspective
6. **JSON for automation** — Use `--format json` to integrate SEO checks into CI/CD pipelines
7. **Focus on meta tags** — Title and description are the highest-impact, lowest-effort SEO wins
8. **Structured data** — JSON-LD validation helps you appear in rich search results
9. **Check social tags** — Open Graph and Twitter Cards improve social media sharing appearance
10. **Regular audits** — Run audits monthly to catch SEO regressions early
