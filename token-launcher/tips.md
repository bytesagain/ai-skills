# Token Launcher — Tips & Tricks 🚀

## Before You Launch

1. **Always use `--dry-run` first** — Generate and review all configs before committing to anything on-chain.
2. **Revoke authorities immediately** after launch — Mint authority and freeze authority should be revoked to build trust.
3. **Lock liquidity** for at least 6 months — Use a reputable locker. This is the #1 trust signal.

## Pump.fun Specific Tips

- Set your token image to 512x512 PNG for best display
- Description should be under 200 characters
- Social links (Twitter, Telegram, website) dramatically increase trust
- Launch during US market hours (9am-5pm EST) for maximum visibility

## Tokenomics Best Practices

- Keep team allocation under 10% — High team allocation is a red flag
- 80%+ to liquidity pool is the gold standard
- If you must have tax, keep it under 3% total (buy + sell)
- Consider a small airdrop allocation (2-5%) for community building

## Security Checklist Priorities

1. ✅ Mint authority revoked
2. ✅ Freeze authority revoked  
3. ✅ Liquidity locked (6-12 months minimum)
4. ✅ No hidden admin functions
5. ✅ Metadata set to immutable

## Common Mistakes

- ❌ Launching with too low liquidity (need at least 5-10 SOL)
- ❌ Not testing on devnet first
- ❌ Setting tax too high (kills trading volume)
- ❌ Forgetting to add metadata (name, image, description)
